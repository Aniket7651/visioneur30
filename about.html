About VISION EUROPE 2030

VISION EUROPE 2030 was a high-level policy conference taking place in Brussels, on September 2nd and 3rd of 2025, organised by the European Alliance for Personalised Medicine (EAPM) and co-funded by the EU4Health Programme.

In a period of rising health disparities, workforce shortages, and digital transition, this conference provided a strategic platform to address the future of healthcare in Europe. It was designed to support the European Commission’s 2024–2029 health priorities by uniting stakeholders around practical policy solutions that drive investment, innovation, and equity.

The event brought together health ministers, EU institutions, regulatory bodies, patient organisations, industry leaders, academics, and clinicians to develop actionable frameworks across diseases, systems, and sectors.

Our Mission

Convene Europe’s healthcare leaders around a unified vision for health system transformation, rooted in equity, data-driven innovation, and measurable outcomes.

Our Vision

A connected European Health Union that delivers sustainable, personalised, and accessible care for every citizen, driven by shared data, trust, and investment

Why Now?

The conference responds to critical challenges:

  • Europe invests €30 billion less annually in health R&D compared to the US.
  • Cancer survival rates vary significantly across Member States.
  • Workforce shortages threaten the delivery of care in oncology, radiology, and rare diseases.
  • The digital transition requires urgent investment in skills, infrastructure, and interoperability.

VISION EUROPE 2030 provides a forum to translate policy ambition into delivery, across borders, sectors, and disease areas.

Purpose and Objectives

VISION EUROPE 2030 aims to:

  • Accelerate the implementation of personalised medicine and digital health tools across EU Member States
  • Promote regulatory alignment and access equity, particularly in cancer, rare diseases, cardiovascular, and neurodegenerative conditions
  • Support the European Health Data Space (EHDS) and streamline clinical trials through AI-powered solutions
  • Foster cross-border partnerships between governments, academia, industry, and civil society
  • Lay the groundwork for long-term reforms in R&D investment, workforce capacity, and data governance

In a world that’s increasingly interconnected and diverse, EAPM is dedicated to spearheading a new era in healthcare — one where precision and personalization redefine the standards of prevention and treatment strategies. Join us in exploring the forefront of medical innovation, where our commitment to individualized care is not just a goal but a revolution.
