Rare Disease Innovation & Cross-Border Access

Fast-Tracking Diagnosis and Treatment Through Policy, Data, and Investment

“For rare disease patients, the gap is not scientific—it's structural.”

Patients with rare diseases often endure 5–7 years to receive a correct diagnosis. New technologies—including AI-based screening, digital phenotyping, and blockchain-based trial platforms—can radically shorten this journey. But fragmented care pathways and insufficient cross-border alignment remain key barriers.

This theme tackles how EU frameworks, venture capital, and SME innovation can converge to build cross-border rare disease trial hubs, shared diagnostics platforms, and real-time patient data infrastructures. It will highlight policy mechanisms that incentivise early diagnosis, improve funding predictability for orphan therapies, and support long-term patient monitoring.

What if we could reduce diagnosis-to-treatment times for rare diseases from years to months?

In a world that’s increasingly interconnected and diverse, EAPM is dedicated to spearheading a new era in healthcare — one where precision and personalization redefine the standards of prevention and treatment strategies. Join us in exploring the forefront of medical innovation, where our commitment to individualized care is not just a goal but a revolution.
