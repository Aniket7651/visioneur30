About the Venue

Club University Foundation

11, Rue d’Egmont 1000 Brussels, Belgium

Located in the heart of the European Quarter, the Club University Foundation (Fondation Universitaire) provides a distinguished setting for high-level dialogue. This historic venue—just steps away from the Royal Palace and EU institutions—offers an ideal environment for strategic policy exchange and multi-stakeholder engagement.


How to Reach the Venue

By Metro

Parc / Park Station (Lines 1 and 5):
Approximately a 5-minute walk via Rue Royale and Rue d’Egmont.
Troon / Trône Station (Lines 2 and 6):
Approximately a 7-minute walk via Rue Montoyer and Rue d’Egmont.

By Train

From Brussels Central Station:
A 10-minute walk or take metro lines 1 or 5 to Parc station.
From Brussels-Midi / Zuid Station:
Take metro lines 2 or 6 to Troon / Trône and continue on foot.

By Brussels Airport

By Train:
Direct train to Brussels Central,
followed by a short walk or metro connection.
By Taxi:
Approximately 30 minutes, average fare around €35.

By Car

Public parking is available nearby, including:

  • Interparking Porte de Namur
  • Albertine-Square Parking

Please note that the venue is located within Brussels’ Low Emission Zone (LEZ). Drivers should verify compliance with local regulations.

Accessibility

The venue is wheelchair accessible. For any specific accessibility needs, please contact the conference secretariat in advance.
