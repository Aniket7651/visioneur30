About VISION Europe 2030

VISION Europe 2030 is a high-level policy conference taking place in Brussels, September 2025, co-organised by the European Alliance for Personalised Medicine (EAPM) and co-funded by the EU4Health Programme.

More...

About The Venue

Located in the heart of the European Quarter, the Club University Foundation (Fondation Universitaire) provides a distinguished setting for high-level dialogue. This historic venue—just steps away from the Royal Palace and EU institutions—offers an ideal environment for strategic policy exchange and multi-stakeholder engagement.

Conference Highlights

A Strategic EU Conference

Aligned with the European Commission's 2024–2029 priorities, VISION Europe 2030 addresses urgent challenges in digital transformation, R&D investment, and equitable access across European health systems.

50+ High-Level Speakers

contributors include leaders from the European Commission, EMA, WHO Europe, ESMO, Guardant Health, and the European Brain Council—alongside patient advocates and AI innovators.

Cross-Disease Focus

scalable innovation in oncology, cardiology, brain health, rare diseases, and chronic conditions. Each track brings forward targeted policy recommendations and use cases.

Precision Medicine in Practice

Session on NGS, AI-powered diagnostics, liquid biopsy, and digital pathology will demonstrate how innovation is transforming early detection, monitoring, and access to care.

Clinical Trials Reimagined

From the “Rome Trial Blueprint” to decentralized designs and blockchain-secure data, we chart how to democratize clinical research and accelerate inclusion.

Health Equity at the Core

Key discussions on joint procurement, reflex testing mandates, and rural access strategies to close Europe’s diagnostic and treatment gap.

Data & Infrastructure

Top regulators and data scientists discuss the future of real-world data, genomic integration, and sustainability in health data infrastructure.

Towards a Shared Agenda

The conference will conclude with the launch of the Declaration for Precision Innovation in European Health Systems, backed by stakeholders from policy, research, and patient communities.

Our Mission

To convene Europe’s healthcare leaders around a unified vision for health system transformation, rooted in equity, data-driven innovation, and measurable outcomes.

In a world that’s increasingly interconnected and diverse, EAPM is dedicated to spearheading a new era in healthcare — one where precision and personalization redefine the standards of prevention and treatment strategies. Join us in exploring the forefront of medical innovation, where our commitment to individualized care is not just a goal but a revolution.
